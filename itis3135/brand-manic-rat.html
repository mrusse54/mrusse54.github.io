Brand Page

Manic Rat is an animal grooming service that treats your dogs like family. We know that you care about your pets health and wellbeing that is why you are looking at our page right now. For this reason we strive to provide the best services we can to our Customers. We offer a stress free enviroment for your pets and our products used are all natural. We also offer services to non-traditional pets

  • Our grooming technitians are pet grooming school certified.
  • We employ stress reducing methods such as grooming slings.
  • Our products used are all natural and gentle of sensitive skin.
  • Customers can bring their own products for their pets medical reasons
  • Customers are welcomed to watch our grooming proccess to insure their pets are being treated properly.
  • We groom non-traditional pets such as hedgehogs, ferrets, and raccoons.
  • Our grooming services are affordable and we offer discounts to new and recuring customers.
